Why play

A galaxy of real rivals

Every battle is against a real player who can fight back, retaliate, or rally their entire fleet against you.

Real-time PvP combat

Three attack types, each with a role: Raid to steal units, Hack to drop their shields, Bomb to wipe out population. Win by outplaying real commanders.

Fleets that matter

Join or create a fleet, donate to the Fleet Vault to unlock perks, and level up together. Coordinated fleets gain combat bonuses solo players simply can't match.

Multi-stage fleet contracts

Cooperative missions with up to 35 stages and millions of HP. Coordinate attacks, manage troop regen, and split the rewards against defenses on a clock.

NPC siege operations

Four-phase assaults on Coalition fleets — cut their supply lines, breach shields, bomb the hull, then board. The deeper you push, the harder it gets and the bigger the payout.

Build your empire

Upgrade your Garrison, Penetration Lab, Bombing Bay, and Shield Generator. Specialize in offense or fortress play, equip gems, and charge your Ion Cannon for a guaranteed strike.

Live social features

Real-time fleet chat, intergalactic chat, direct messages, wall posts, and a friends list. Coordinate strikes, trash-talk rivals, or just hang out between battles.

The combat loop

Three ways to break a rival

Each attack counters a different defense. Read your target, spend troops wisely, and strike before they rally.

Steal their units

Send raid troops against their turrets to loot the units that fund your war machine.

Step 2 · Hack

Drop the shield

Burn through their firewall with hacks until the shield hits zero and they're exposed.

Step 3 · Bomb

Wipe the population

With the shield down, bombs tear through population — the heart of their total power.

A few of the most common questions — the full guide lives in our FAQ.

Is Galactic Conquest free to play?

Yes. It's free to download and play on iOS and Android. Optional in-app purchases (such as Federal Crystals and gems) are available, but you earn currency through raids, contracts, and daily challenges.

How do I get started?

Complete the Training Tracks in the Academy — they teach raiding, hacking, bombing, and fleet management. Then head to Intergalactic Chat and find a player-run fleet to join. A fleet behind you makes a huge difference.

How does combat work?

There are three attack types. Raid steals units (your raid power vs their turrets). Hack damages their shield (your hack power vs their firewall). Once the shield is down, Bomb destroys their population (your bomb power vs their bombing dome).

What is a fleet?

A fleet is a group of players who work together — contribute units, earn fleet XP, and unlock shared assets like the Dyson Sphere and Medical Bay. After 5 consecutive days, a fleet becomes your home fleet and you can join its fleet wars.
