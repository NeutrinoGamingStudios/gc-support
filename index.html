How to Delete Your Account

If you wish to permanently remove your account and all associated data, follow these steps:

  1. 1
    Open Galactic Conquest and navigate to Settings
  2. 2
    Select Administration from the settings menu
  3. 3
    Choose "Delete Account" option
⚠️ Important: This action will permanently delete your account and all stored data, including your email, password, and in-app purchase history. This action cannot be undone.
